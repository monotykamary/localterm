Add GitHub-style multiline comments to the diff viewer: press and hold a line's comment button and drag across lines (in either direction, in unified or split view) to select a range, then release to comment on it. The range highlights while dragging and on saved comments, Escape cancels an in-progress drag, and review prompts sent to the terminal reference the full line span (e.g. `src/main.ts L10-L14`)
